About me

I'm currently a Researcher at Zhongguancun Laboratory. I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University in 2016, under the supervision of Prof. Mingwei Xu. I received my bachelor degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2011. I was a visiting Ph.D. student at University of Surrey (host: Prof. Ning Wang) from 2014.4 to 2014.9. Prior to joining the ZGC Lab, I worked as a researcher at the Research and Development Center, Agricultural Bank of China.

My research interests include computer network architecture, routing lookup, network security, and network measurement.
